Unmatched Ventures Deals

Convoy (pre-seed) -> entry point 5.5 Post-Money Val. I recruited a senior executive from Square to join as a co-founder. They are about to raise at a 5X from tier-1 VCs. I also helped them create their GTM strategy.


Convoy is a marketplace helping SMBs leverage group-buying power to compete with larger enterprises. The Convoy Discount Network is a plug and play tech solution for SMB hubs including Chambers of Commerce, VC portfolios, and trade unions to aggregate and provide discounts from and for their members.


What I liked about the deal:

Strong Founder-Market Fit: CEO Yasin Abbak is a third-time founder with a successful exit under his belt. Yassin began his career in finance (JP Morgan and Morgan Stanley) and understands commerce, financial markets, and business operations extremely well. His father ran and operated a SMB and Yasin is aware of the pain points SMB owners face.


Minimal Competition: While there are many Group Purchasing Organizations (GPOs) most organizations do not address SMB supply chain inequalities. Additionally, using Chambers of Commerce as a GTM wedge is brilliant as Chambers have in-house sales teams and networks and this helps keep CAC low.


Strong Synergies: Convoy can provide valuable savings to our existing portfolio companies, increasing the capital efficiency of our other deployed capital. Convoy’s network as it scales opens up opportunities for our SAAS product investments to sell and test services directly to SMBs which is otherwise an expensive market to reach.


Large and growing TAM: In 2020, the number of small businesses in the US reached 31.7 million, making up nearly all (99.9 percent) US businesses. This is also representative of the sustained growth as it marks a 3.15 percent increase from the previous year and a growth of 7.09 percent over the three-year period from 2017 to 2020. SMBs generate 44% of the US Economy.


Proven Pain Point: Small and Medium Size Businesses Lose 22% a Year Without Group Purchasing Organization (GPO) Buying Power.


Market-ready conditions: Amid the post-COVID "new normal," businesses of all sizes are adopting new work patterns and innovating to save time, money, and resources. Against this backdrop, Convoy arrives with a new purchasing option for many: augmenting the group purchasing organization (GPO) supply chain that can save SMBs considerable money and keeping costs low. There are not too many direct competitors in the market meaning that if Convoy does this right they can capture considerable market share.


Strong Capital Efficiency: The company and team has made substantial progress given the amount of capital raised to date.
